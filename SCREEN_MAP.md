# Screen Map

## Public

- `/`: marketing entry
- `/sign-in`: authentication
- `/sign-up`: authentication
- `/g/[qr]`: guest table menu

## App

- `/app`: overview
- `/app/launch-setup`: guided launch flow
- `/app/tables`: tables and QR registry
- `/app/menu`: menu management
- `/app/live`: live sessions
- `/app/settings`: system and payment settings

## Expected behavior

- Overview shows launch and live health
- Launch Setup reduces setup to the minimum required steps
- Tables & QR focuses on operational table identity
- Menu focuses on versioned pricing
- Live Sessions focuses on claims and payment readiness
